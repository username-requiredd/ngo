Home Detail

The purpose of light foundation center for peace co-existence and Development] is to provide essential humanitarian services aimed at improving the quality of life for communities in need. Our focus areas include the provision of quality education, access to clean water, sanitation and hygiene (WASH) services, improving healthcare access, facilitating livelihood opportunities and initiatives addressing climate change and resilience. By addressing these fundamental needs, we aim to create sustainable and positive impact, empowering individuals and communities to reach their full potential."

The mission of light foundation center for peace co-existence and Development] typically reflects its commitment to alleviating human suffering, protecting human dignity, and providing support to vulnerable populations affected by crises. Saving Lives and Alleviating Suffering: To respond swiftly to emergencies, whether natural disasters, conflicts, or other crises, providing immediate relief in the form of food, water, shelter, and medical care.

Promoting Human Dignity: To ensure that people affected by crises are treated with dignity and respect, safeguarding their rights and ensuring equitable access to resources and assistance. Supporting Sustainable Development: To work towards long-term solutions that reduce vulnerability, strengthen local resilience, and empower communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods. Neutrality, Impartiality, and Independence: To provide aid without discrimination, regardless of race, religion, nationality, or political affiliation, and maintain independence from political, economic, or military interests.
